Kineto Profiler
==========================================

## Introduction

The xpu kineto profiler has not been maintained in Intel® Extension for PyTorch* since Intel® Extension for PyTorch* 2.5 release, please use profiler from PyTorch instead. For the profiler usage, please refer to the official torch profiler tutorial [PyTorch\*'s tutorial page](https://pytorch.org/tutorials/recipes/recipes/profiler_recipe.html).
